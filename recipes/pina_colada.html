Mango Pina Colada Smoothie

Description

A piña colada is a sweet, tropical cocktail typically made with rum, pineapple juice, and cream of coconut or coconut milk, shaken or blended with ice.
It originated in Puerto Rico and is often garnished with a pineapple wedge and/or a maraschino cherry.

This smoothie is my take on an alcohol-free Pina-Colada.

Ingredients

  • 1 mango - peeled, seeded, and cubed
  • 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
  • 1 ¼ cups ice cubes
  • 1 ¼ cups pineapple juice
  • ½ cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Directions

  1. Blend mango, coconut milk, ice, pineapple juice, cream and sugar until smooth.
  2. Pour into glasses and serve.
